MANAGEMENT   TEAM

SJB Hospitality Services is one of the growing services provider in Hospitality industry,
spreading all over Maharashtra, taking care of corporate needs as well as leisure travels.

SJB Hospitality services established in 2010 and headquartered in Goregaon East, Mumbai, Maharashtra.

SJB Hospitality Services management team consist of 2 partners namely Mr. Jay Bhanushali & Mrs. Saniya Jain &
a team of 10 headquartered in Mumbai. The total strength of 50,which give operational help and administrations
to all the distinctive area of the property.





Mr. Jay Bhanushali
Owner
Jay Bhanushali is the founder of Alliance Travels and SJB Hospitality Services. Jay’s diverse experience of 20 years includes car rentals, service apartments and Hotel management. He is a big stickler for detail and a serial entrepreneur. His aim is to provide discerning business and leisure travelers with the highest standard of luxury hospitality. He travels, mentors aspiring entrepreneurs in his spare time, is a huge movie buff and enjoys travel, music and a game of cricket (both as a player and a spectator) every now and then. Jay obtained his graduation from KJSCE.



Ms. Saniya Jain
Owner
Saniya graduated from KJSCE. She is the co founder of SJB Hospitality Services. Prior to founding SJB, she was successfully running her Insurance business for 7 years. Saniya is very passionate about Travel and Music as it brings people together to explore life and world. Her dedication, eagerness to learn, adapt and embrace the future are key assets to the organization. She enjoys good food and globetrotting.
